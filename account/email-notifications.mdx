---
title: "Email Notifications"
description: "Automatic emails Rendio sends to your account email address for quota, billing, and account events."
---

Rendio sends transactional emails to the address on your account. All notifications are currently always-on — there are no per-notification preferences yet.

## Notification types

### Quota & billing

| Email | When it fires | Plans |
|---|---|---|
| **Quota warning (80%)** | First time you reach 80% of your monthly quota in a billing period | All plans |
| **Quota reached (100%)** | When your monthly quota is exhausted and renders start returning `429` | All plans |
| **Spending cap reached** | When overage charges hit your configured cap and renders are blocked | Starter, Pro (overage enabled) |
| **Overage billing summary** | After Paddle charges overage at the end of a billing period | Starter, Pro (overage enabled) |
| **Plan updated** | Upgrade, downgrade, or cancellation confirmation | All plans |

### Onboarding

| Email | When it fires |
|---|---|
| **First render success** | Sent once, after your first successful render |
| **Early errors alert** | Sent if you have 5 or more `4xx` errors in your first 7 days with zero successful renders |

The early errors alert is designed to catch misconfigured API keys, missing `Content-Type` headers, or other setup issues that prevent you from completing a first render.

## Deduplication

Quota warning and quota reached emails are sent **once per billing period**. If you upgrade mid-period and your usage drops below the new quota, you won't receive a second warning until the next period.

## Updating your email address

Your notification email is the address on your Rendio account. Update it in **Dashboard → Settings → Profile**.
